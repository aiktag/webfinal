Blizzard Entertainment


A universe originally invented for computer games from the Warcraft series. Later it was also developed into board games, a trading card game, books, comics, films, various marketing products such as action figures, T-shirts, mouse pads, etc. World of Warcraft magazine is published on a quarterly basis: The Magazine. Like many other popular fictional universes, the Warcraft universe is enriched by unofficial creativity: comics, video, including the full-length film “Tales of the Past” in three parts, made on the World of Warcraft engine by a student Martin Fall.
